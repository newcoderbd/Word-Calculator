Privacy Policy

Effective Date: November 06, 2025

Your privacy is our priority.
WordCalculator.net is a 100% client-side web tool designed for writers, students, and professionals. Every feature — word count, character count, keyword density, grammar simulation, and text tools — runs entirely in your browser. We do not collect, store, or transmit any of your text or personal data. This policy explains everything in clear, simple terms.

1. About Word Calculator

WordCalculator.net is a free, open-source-inspired online writing assistant. Our mission is to provide powerful, accurate, and privacy-first tools without ads, tracking, or data harvesting.

No account needed. No login. No data shared.

2. Data We Do NOT Collect

We are proud to operate with zero data collection. Specifically:

  • No personal information (name, email, etc.)
  • No IP address logging
  • No cookies for tracking or analytics
  • No text from your editor is ever sent to our servers
  • No usage data is stored or shared
  • No third-party scripts that track you

Your writing stays 100% on your device.

3. How Your Text Is Processed

All calculations (word count, reading time, keyword density, etc.) happen instantly in your browser using JavaScript. When you close the tab:

  • All text is erased from memory
  • No backup is created on our end
  • Nothing is logged

This is the same technology used in apps like Notepad — but with advanced writing stats.

4. Auto-Save (Optional & Local)

If you enable "Auto-Save":

  • Your draft is saved in your browser's localStorage
  • It never leaves your device
  • Deleted automatically when you click "Clear"
  • Cleared if you clear browser data

No cloud sync. No account. No risk.

5. Third-Party Services (Current & Future)

Currently: We use no external APIs. All features are self-contained.

In the future: If we add optional grammar checking (e.g., LanguageTool, Grammarly):

  • It will be clearly labeled
  • You must click "Check Grammar" to activate
  • Only then will text be sent to the chosen service
  • You will see a notice: "This sends text to [Service]"
  • Their privacy policy will apply to that action

6. Compliance with Privacy Laws

We fully comply with:

  • GDPR (EU) – No data processed
  • CCPA/CPRA (California) – No sale of data
  • COPPA – Safe for children
  • CalOPPA – Transparent policy

You have the right to know we collect nothing.

7. Security

Since no data is transmitted or stored, there is:

  • No database to hack
  • No server logs
  • No risk of data breach

We use HTTPS and modern web standards for safety.

8. Children's Privacy

WordCalculator.net is safe and appropriate for all ages, including children under 13. We collect zero data, so COPPA compliance is inherent.

9. Changes to This Policy

We will only update this policy if we add new features that affect privacy. You will always see:

  • Clear "Last Updated" date
  • Summary of changes
  • Continued use = acceptance
