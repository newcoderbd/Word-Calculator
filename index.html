What is Word Calculator?

Word Calculator is a fast, accurate writing workspace designed to help you measure and improve your text in one place. Paste or type your content to see live counts for words, characters, sentences, and paragraphs, along with estimated reading and speaking time. You also get keyword frequency and density to balance repetition and strengthen clarity.

Beyond counting, Word Calculator includes practical editing helpers—case converters (Title, Sentence, UPPER/lower), smart cleanup, find-and-replace, TXT/MD export, printing, and optional grammar feedback. You can also listen to your writing with text-to-speech to catch awkward phrasing.

Whether you’re drafting an essay, polishing a blog post, or preparing professional copy, Word Calculator gives you the metrics, clarity, and tools you need to write with confidence—no sign-up required.

Note: Automated suggestions provide guidance; always review tone and context before publishing.
